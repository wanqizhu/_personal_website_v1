Books hold a special place in my heart, transcending time and place and occasion. Some stories fill my mind with wonder and my heart with a deeply human sense of understanding, a reminder of what's important in this transcient current of life. Other writngs are sharp and clear, cutting across the mist of unverbalized unorganized thoughts and enlightening my mind with a fresh perspective. If you're looking for an adventure or a dose of inspiration, here are some of my most memorable readings.
